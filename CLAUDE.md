## Reviewing PRs

Before reviewing, looking over, or commenting on any PR, ALWAYS first look for a
documented reviewer workflow and follow it instead of an ad-hoc review. Check,
in order: `docs/reviewer-role.md`, the "Reviewing PRs" section in `AGENTS.md`,
then any `CONTRIBUTING.md` / `.github/` review guidance. Run the PR through that
workflow's checklist and use its verdict format.
